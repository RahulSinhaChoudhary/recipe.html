Idly

Ingredients

rice, urad dal, fenugreek seed, salt

Process

Grind rice and fenugreek seed, add salt, steam idlis.

Dosa

Ingredients

rice, semolina, urad dal, fenugreek seed, salt, oil

Process

Grind rice, dal and fenugreek seeds and mix, add semolina to batter, put laddleful of batter, cook on medium flame.

Upma

Ingredients

steamed flour, coconut, groundnut, onion, green chillied, curry leaves, mustuard seed, urad dal, chana dal, lemon juice, salt, oil, coriander leaves

Process

Mix flour, coconut, groundnut, fry onion and chillies in little oil, add salt and water, heat oil in a pan.

Sweet pongal

Ingredients

moong dal, rice, jaggery, milk, ghee, cardamoms, raisins, cashewnut

Process

wash rice and dal and cook it in milk, add jaggery and ghee and cook.
